## Executive Summary

The automotive service industry in Saudi Arabia and the wider MENA region represents a multi-billion dollar market facing digital transformation pressure. SALIS AUTO provides a comprehensive, compliant, and AI-powered ERP platform that eliminates the operational inefficiencies costing garages an estimated 20–35% of potential revenue annually.

---

## Problem Statement

### Current State of Automotive Garage Operations

Most automotive garages — even large operations — face a fragmented technology landscape:

| Problem | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Paper-based job cards | Lost information, delays, errors |
| Manual inventory tracking | Stockouts, overstocking, theft |
| Disconnected billing | Revenue leakage, late payments |
| No customer self-service | High CSR overhead, low satisfaction |
| Manual compliance (VAT, ZATCA) | Fines, audit risk |
| No data analytics | Blind decision-making |
| Disconnected technicians | Inefficiency, low accountability |
| No predictive maintenance alerts | Customer churn, missed upsell |
| Manual scheduling | Bay under-utilization |
| No parts network | Expensive emergency sourcing |

### Financial Impact of Inefficiency
- Average garage loses **15–25%** of labor capacity to administrative overhead
- Stockout events cost garages **8–12%** of potential parts revenue
- Manual invoicing errors affect **3–5%** of transactions
- Customer no-shows (no reminder system) represent **10–15%** of appointment slots

## Solution: SALIS AUTO Platform

### Core Value Propositions

#### 1. Operational Unification
A single platform replaces 10–15 disconnected tools:
- Job management software
- Inventory/ERP system
- Billing/invoicing tool
- Accounting software
- HR/payroll system
- CRM/customer database
- Scheduling tool
- Compliance/VAT tools

**Estimated Savings:** SAR 15,000–40,000/year in software licensing

#### 2. Revenue Protection
- Automated appointment reminders reduce no-shows by **60–70%**
- Smart parts recommendations increase service upsell by **15–25%**
- Loyalty program increases repeat customer visits by **30–45%**
- AI pricing optimization improves margins by **5–10%**

#### 3. Labor Efficiency
- Service bay dashboard reduces idle time by **20–30%**
- Smart job assignment reduces scheduling overhead by **40%**
- Technician mobile app eliminates paper job cards (saves **45 min/tech/day**)
- Automated reordering eliminates manual purchasing cycles (**3–5 hours/week**)

#### 4. Compliance Risk Elimination
- ZATCA-certified e-invoicing eliminates compliance risk
- Automated VAT calculation on all transactions
- Zakat computation for Islamic business compliance
- Audit trail for every transaction

#### 5. Customer Experience Uplift
- Real-time vehicle tracking via public URL
- Customer portal for self-service
- AI chatbot for 24/7 customer support
- Video estimates and consultations
- Loyalty program with tiered rewards
