**Document Type:** Training Manual

## Introduction

Welcome to SALIS AUTO's Customer Portal! This guide explains how to use your online account to book appointments, track your vehicle's service status, view invoices, and manage your loyalty points.

You don't need to be technical to use the portal — it's designed to be simple and straightforward.

---

## Chapter 1: Getting Started

### 1.1 Accessing the Customer Portal
You can access the customer portal in two ways:
- **Desktop/laptop:** Visit the main SALIS AUTO website and click "Customer Login"
- **Mobile phone:** Use the mobile-optimized app at `/customer-app`
- **Direct link:** Your garage will provide your portal URL

### 1.2 Logging In
1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password (given to you by the garage when you registered)
3. Click **Login**
4. You'll see your personal dashboard

**Forgot your password?** Contact the garage reception or call center for a password reset.

### 1.3 Your Dashboard Overview
After logging in, your dashboard shows:
- **My Vehicles** — vehicles registered under your account
- **Upcoming Appointments** — scheduled service dates
- **Active Jobs** — vehicles currently being serviced
- **Outstanding Invoices** — any unpaid bills
- **Loyalty Points** — your current reward balance

## Chapter 2: Booking an Appointment

### 2.1 New Appointment (Mobile App: `/customer-app/booking`)
1. Tap **Book Appointment** from your home screen
2. Select the vehicle you want serviced
3. Choose the type of service needed:
   - Oil Change
   - Tire Service
   - Brake Service
   - General Inspection
   - Other (describe the issue)
4. Select your preferred date and time from available slots
5. Add any notes (e.g., "strange noise when braking")
6. Tap **Confirm Booking**
7. You receive a confirmation notification

### 2.2 Managing Appointments
- **View appointments:** Dashboard → My Appointments
- **Reschedule:** Click the appointment → Edit Date/Time
- **Cancel:** Click the appointment → Cancel (give at least 2 hours notice)

## Chapter 3: Tracking Your Vehicle

### 3.1 Live Service Status
When your vehicle is at the garage, track its progress:

**Option 1: Public Tracking Link**
- The garage will send you a tracking link via SMS (e.g., `https://salis.app/track/abc123`)
- Click the link — **no login required**
- See real-time status updates

**Option 2: Customer Portal**
1. Log in to the portal
2. Go to **My Jobs** or **Live Tracking**
3. See current status: Pending → In Progress → Quality Check → Ready for Pickup

### 3.2 Status Meanings
| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| Pending | Vehicle received, awaiting technician |
| In Progress | Technician actively working on vehicle |
| On Hold | Waiting for parts or approval |
| Quality Check | Work complete, being inspected |
| Ready for Pickup | Vehicle is ready to collect |
| Delivered | Vehicle has been returned to you |

### 3.3 Receiving Updates
Updates are sent via:
- **SMS** (Arabic or English based on your preference)
- **Email** notifications
- **In-app** notifications in the portal

## Chapter 4: Viewing Your Vehicles

### 4.1 My Vehicles (`/client/vehicles`)
View all vehicles registered under your account:
- Make, model, year, color
- License plate number
- Current mileage
- Last service date
- Next service due

### 4.2 Service History (`/client/service-history`)
For each vehicle, see:
- Every service performed with date and cost
- Parts replaced
- Technician who performed the work
- Mileage at each service
- Invoices for each visit

### 4.3 Adding a New Vehicle
Contact the garage reception to add a new vehicle — they will verify your ownership and add it to your account.

## Chapter 5: Invoices & Payments

### 5.1 Viewing Invoices (`/client/invoices`)
All your invoices are stored digitally:
1. Go to **My Invoices**
2. See all invoices: paid, unpaid, draft
3. Click any invoice to view details
4. Print or download as PDF

### 5.2 Online Payment
For unpaid invoices:
1. Click the invoice
2. Click **Pay Now**
3. Select payment method:
   - Credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard) via Stripe
   - PayPal
4. Complete payment
5. Receive digital receipt

### 5.3 VAT Receipt
All invoices include:
- VAT amount (15%)
- ZATCA QR code for verification
- Your TRN if applicable

## Chapter 6: Loyalty Program

### 6.1 How Points Work
- Earn **1 point per SAR 10** spent on services
- Points accumulate across all visits
- Points never expire (while account is active)

### 6.2 Loyalty Tiers
| Tier | Points Required | Benefits |
|------|-----------------|---------|
| Bronze | 0–999 | 5% discount on parts |
| Silver | 1,000–4,999 | 10% discount + priority booking |
| Gold | 5,000–9,999 | 15% discount + free oil change/year |
| Platinum | 10,000+ | 20% discount + dedicated advisor |

### 6.3 Redeeming Points
1. When booking a service, you'll see your available points
2. Choose to apply points as a discount
3. 100 points = SAR 10 discount
4. Points are deducted after service completion

## Chapter 7: Appointment Reminders

### 7.1 Service Reminders
SALIS AUTO automatically reminds you when service is due:
- **Email reminder** 2 weeks before due date
- **SMS reminder** 3 days before due date
- **In-app notification** on the day

### 7.2 Managing Reminders
1. Go to **My Reminders** (`/client/reminders`)
2. View upcoming service reminders
3. Configure your preferred contact method (email/SMS)
4. Set reminder timing preferences

## Chapter 8: Reviews & Feedback

### 8.1 Rating Your Service
After job completion:
1. You receive a feedback request via email/SMS
2. Rate your experience (1–5 stars)
3. Write optional comments
4. Your feedback helps improve service quality

### 8.2 Chat with Your Advisor (`/client/review-chat`)
For any questions about your recent service:
1. Go to **Chat with Us**
2. Send your message
3. Your service advisor responds within business hours

## Chapter 9: Referral Program

### 9.1 How Referrals Work
Refer a friend and both of you benefit:
1. Go to your profile → **Referral Program**
2. Copy your unique referral link
3. Share with friends/family
4. When they complete their first service, you earn 500 bonus points
5. They receive 10% off their first service

## Tips for Getting the Best Experience

- **Book in advance** — popular time slots fill up fast
- **Describe your issue clearly** when booking — helps technicians prepare
- **Track your vehicle** via the link we send — no need to call for updates
- **Keep the app bookmarked** for quick access
- **Enable notifications** to stay informed on service status
