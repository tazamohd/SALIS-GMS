# SALIS AUTO — Glossary of Terms

**Document Type:** Knowledge Base — Glossary  
**Last Updated:** March 2026  

---

## A

**Accounts Payable (AP)**  
Money owed by the garage to suppliers and vendors for parts and services received but not yet paid.

**Accounts Receivable (AR)**  
Money owed to the garage by customers for services rendered but not yet paid.

**AI Scheduling**  
Artificial intelligence algorithm that analyzes technician availability, job complexity, and bay availability to recommend optimal appointment times.

**Appointment**  
A scheduled time slot for a customer to bring their vehicle for service. Appointments convert to job cards when the vehicle arrives.

**AR (Augmented Reality)**  
Technology that overlays digital information on the real world. Used in SALIS AUTO for repair guides, showing technicians step-by-step instructions overlaid on the physical vehicle.

**Auto-Reorder**  
Automated system that monitors inventory levels and creates purchase orders when stock falls below the minimum threshold.

---

## B

**Balance Sheet**  
Financial statement showing assets, liabilities, and equity at a specific point in time.

**Bay / Service Bay**  
Physical workshop space where a vehicle is serviced. SALIS AUTO tracks bay occupancy in real time.

**B2B Parts Network**  
A business-to-business marketplace where network member garages can request and supply spare parts to each other.

**Blockchain Record**  
An immutable, verifiable record of a vehicle's service history stored on a distributed blockchain ledger.

**Branch**  
A physical location belonging to a garage. Larger operations may have multiple branches.

---

## C

**Chart of Accounts**  
The master list of all financial accounts used in the accounting system (assets, liabilities, equity, income, expenses).

**Clock In / Clock Out**  
The process of recording when a technician starts and ends their work shift, used for payroll calculation.

**Compliance**  
Adherence to regulatory requirements. In Saudi Arabia: ZATCA e-invoicing, VAT, Zakat, and safety regulations.

**CRM (Customer Relationship Management)**  
System for managing customer interactions, history, and relationships. Includes loyalty programs and feedback.

**CSR (Customer Service Representative)**  
Staff role responsible for handling customer inquiries, scheduling, and front-line support.

---

## D

**Dashboard**  
The main overview screen for each role, displaying key metrics, recent activity, and quick actions.

**Demand Forecasting**  
AI-powered prediction of future parts demand based on historical usage patterns, seasonal trends, and scheduled maintenance.

**Digital Twin**  
A virtual digital replica of a physical vehicle that mirrors its real-world condition for remote monitoring.

**Drag-and-Drop Scheduling**  
The Workshop Calendar feature that allows managers to reschedule jobs by dragging them to new time slots or technicians.

**Drizzle ORM**  
The Object-Relational Mapping library used to interact with the PostgreSQL database in a type-safe way.

---

## E

**E-Invoice / E-Invoicing**  
Electronic invoice submitted to ZATCA (Saudi Arabia's tax authority) in a standardized XML format.

**ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)**  
Integrated software system managing all core business processes in a unified platform.

**Estimate**  
A quote provided to a customer before service, showing expected costs for labor and parts.

---

## F

**Fleet**  
A collection of vehicles owned by a single business customer (e.g., delivery company, government agency).

**Franchise**  
A business model where multiple garages operate under a single brand and management structure.

**Fraud Detection**  
ML-powered system that analyzes transaction patterns to identify potentially fraudulent billing or inventory manipulation.

---

## G

**Garage**  
The top-level business entity in SALIS AUTO. A garage can have multiple branches.

**General Ledger**  
The complete record of all financial transactions for an organization.

**Globalization Layer**  
The module managing multi-currency, multi-language, and multi-country settings for international operations.

---

## H

**Hijri Calendar**  
The Islamic lunar calendar used in Saudi Arabia alongside the Gregorian calendar.

**HR (Human Resources)**  
The department and module managing employee profiles, payroll, leave, and training.

---

## I

**Inventory**  
Stock of spare parts held in the warehouse or branch for use in repairs.

**Invoice**  
A bill issued to a customer after service is complete, showing itemized charges for labor and parts.

**IoT (Internet of Things)**  
Network of physical sensors and devices (e.g., workshop temperature, oil pressure monitors) connected to the SALIS AUTO platform.

---

## J

**Job Card**  
The central operational document tracking all work performed on a vehicle from intake to delivery. Contains assigned technician, tasks, parts used, costs, and status.

**Journal Entry**  
A record of a financial transaction in the accounting system, following double-entry bookkeeping rules.

---

## L

**Lead Technician**  
A senior technician role with responsibilities for quality review, task assignment, and junior technician mentoring.

**Loyalty Program**  
Customer rewards system where points are earned for spending and redeemed for discounts. Tiers: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum.

**LTV (Lifetime Value)**  
Total revenue a garage expects to earn from a customer over their entire relationship.

---

## M

**Maintenance Schedule**  
Predefined service intervals (e.g., oil change every 5,000 km) tracked per vehicle.

**Mileage**  
The odometer reading recorded at each service visit, used for maintenance scheduling.

**Multi-Tenant**  
Architecture where multiple garages share the same platform but their data is completely isolated.

---

## N

**Network Member**  
A garage that has joined the B2B Parts Supply Network, enabling parts requests and quotations with other members.

---

## O

**OBD (On-Board Diagnostics)**  
Standardized vehicle diagnostic interface. SALIS AUTO can connect to OBD devices to retrieve fault codes.

**OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)**  
Vehicle manufacturers (BMW, Toyota, etc.) who produce official parts and software. SALIS AUTO manages OEM software subscriptions.

---

## P

**Parts Marketplace**  
Online marketplace within SALIS AUTO where garages can browse and purchase spare parts from multiple suppliers.

**Payroll Run**  
The periodic process of calculating and processing employee salaries.

**Predictive Maintenance**  
AI analysis of vehicle data and service history to predict and prevent future mechanical failures.

**Purchase Order (PO)**  
A formal document sent to a supplier to order goods (spare parts, tools) at agreed pricing.

---

## Q

**QC Inspector**  
Role responsible for quality control inspections before vehicle delivery.

**Quotation**  
A formal price offer in response to a parts request in the B2B network.

---

## R

**RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)**  
Security system where each user's access is determined by their assigned role.

**Referral Program**  
Marketing program where existing customers earn rewards for referring new customers.

**RTL (Right-to-Left)**  
Text direction used in Arabic language, opposite to English's left-to-right direction.

---

## S

**Service Template**  
Predefined task and parts list for common services, applied to job cards for efficiency.

**SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)**  
Unique identifier for each distinct part or product in inventory.

**SLA (Service Level Agreement)**  
Contractual commitment on service quality, response times, and delivery standards.

**Smart Assignment**  
AI-powered job assignment that matches available technicians to jobs based on skills, certifications, and workload.

---

## T

**Technician**  
Workshop staff who perform vehicle repairs and maintenance.

**Telematics**  
Technology for remotely monitoring vehicle data (speed, location, engine health) via cellular or GPS connection.

**Timesheet**  
Record of hours worked by an employee, used for payroll and billing.

**TRN (Tax Registration Number)**  
Saudi Arabia's tax registration identifier required for VAT compliance.

---

## V

**VAT (Value Added Tax)**  
Saudi Arabia's 15% consumption tax applied to goods and services.

**VIN (Vehicle Identification Number)**  
Unique 17-character code identifying every motor vehicle, encoding make, model, year, and serial number.

**VR (Virtual Reality)**  
Immersive 3D environment. Used in SALIS AUTO's VR Showroom for virtual vehicle presentations.

---

## W

**WebSocket**  
Technology enabling real-time two-way communication between the browser and server. Used for live chat, bay monitoring, and notifications.

---

## Z

**Zakat**  
Islamic mandatory charitable contribution calculated on business assets. SALIS AUTO includes Zakat calculation tools for Saudi businesses.

**ZATCA (Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority)**  
Saudi Arabia's tax authority, responsible for e-invoicing mandate, VAT, Zakat, and customs regulations.
