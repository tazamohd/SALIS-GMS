# Screen Documentation — Section 13: Accounting & Finance

**Screens:** 145–171  
**Section:** Accounting & Finance  
**Navigation Group:** Multiple  

---

## Overview

SALIS AUTO features a complete double-entry accounting system covering the full financial lifecycle of a garage business.

---

## Core Accounting Screens (145–154)

### Screen 145 — Chart of Accounts (`/chart-of-accounts`)
Master account structure. See [Accountant Guide](../05-training/accountant-guide.md) for full details.

### Screen 146 — General Ledger (`/general-ledger`)
Complete transaction record by account. Filter by account, date range, reference.

### Screen 147 — Journal Entries (`/journal-entries`)
Post manual accounting entries with debit/credit pairs.

### Screen 148 — Trial Balance (`/trial-balance`)
All account balances at period end. Must balance (debits = credits).

### Screen 149 — Balance Sheet (`/balance-sheet`)
Assets = Liabilities + Equity snapshot.

### Screen 150 — Income Statement (`/income-statement`)
Revenue minus expenses = Net Income for the period.

### Screen 151 — Cash Flow Statement (`/cash-flow-statement`)
Operating, investing, and financing cash movements.

### Screen 152 — Accounts Receivable (`/accounts-receivable`)
Outstanding customer invoices with aging analysis (30/60/90/90+ days).

### Screen 153 — Accounts Payable (`/accounts-payable`)
Outstanding supplier bills with due date tracking.

### Screen 154 — Bank Account Management (`/bank-account-management`)
All bank accounts with reconciliation status.

## Operations Finance (164–171)

### Screen 164 — Expense Tracking (`/expense-tracking`)
Record and categorize all business expenses.

**Expense Categories:**
- Utilities (electricity, water, internet)
- Consumables (cleaning, workshop materials)
- Marketing costs
- Vehicle expenses
- Training and licensing

### Screen 165 — Expenses Management (`/expenses-management`)
Approval workflow for expense claims.

**Approval Flow:**
```
Staff submits expense claim (with receipt photo)
→ Manager approves/rejects
→ Finance records approved expense
→ Reimbursement included in payroll
```

### Screen 166 — Sales Management (`/sales-management`)
Sales performance tracking and analysis.

### Screen 167 — Accounting Integration (`/accounting-integration`)
Connect to external accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, ZATCA).

### Screen 168 — Financial Settings (`/financial-settings`)
- Fiscal year configuration
- VAT settings
- Currency and exchange rates
- Payment terms defaults
- Invoice numbering format

### Screen 169 — Warranty Management (`/warranty-management`)
Track warranties on parts and labor.

**Warranty Records:**
- Part warranty: 6–24 months typical
- Labor warranty: 30 days standard
- Claim process: customer reports issue → compare with warranty terms → repair/replace

### Screen 170 — Contract Management (`/contract-management`)
Fleet and business contracts with SLA tracking.

### Screen 171 — Insurance Claims (`/insurance-claims`)
Process and track vehicle insurance claims.
