## Production Guidelines

### Visual Standards
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Branding: SALIS AUTO watermark bottom-right

### Audio Standards
- Background music: Low, professional
- Voice: Clear, moderate pace
- Sound effects: Subtle UI sounds

### Annotations
- Highlight boxes: Electric blue (#3b82f6)
- Arrows: White with shadow
- Text overlays: Poppins font

### Callout Types
- TIP: Green background, lightbulb icon
- WARNING: Yellow background, triangle icon
- FEATURE: Blue background, star icon
