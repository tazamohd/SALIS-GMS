# SALIS AUTO - Quick Start Training Guide

**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Level:** Beginner  
**Last Updated:** November 3, 2025

---

## 🚀 Welcome to SALIS AUTO!

This quick start guide will get you up and running in 30 minutes.

---

## Part 1: First Steps (5 minutes)

### 1. Login to the System

1. Open your browser and go to your SALIS AUTO URL
2. Click **Login** button
3. Enter your credentials provided by your administrator
4. Click **Sign In**

✅ **Success:** You should see the Dashboard

### 2. Explore the Dashboard

**What you'll see:**
- **Revenue Today:** Current day's revenue
- **Active Jobs:** Jobs currently in progress
- **Pending Appointments:** Upcoming scheduled appointments
- **Low Stock Alerts:** Parts running low

**Try it:** Click around the dashboard to explore different sections

---

## Part 2: Create Your First Customer (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Navigate to Customers

1. Click **Customers** in the left sidebar
2. You'll see a list of existing customers

### Step 2: Add a New Customer

1. Click **+ Add Customer** button (top right)
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Name:** John Doe
   - **Email:** john@example.com
   - **Phone:** +1 (555) 123-4567
   - **Address:** 123 Main St
   - **City:** Riyadh
   - **State/Province:** Riyadh Region
   - **ZIP Code:** 12345
3. Click **Create Customer**

✅ **Success:** Customer appears in the list

---

## Part 3: Add a Vehicle (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Navigate to Vehicles

1. Click **Vehicles** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add Vehicle** button

### Step 2: Enter Vehicle Details

1. **Customer:** Select "John Doe" (from dropdown)
2. **Make:** Toyota
3. **Model:** Camry
4. **Year:** 2020
5. **VIN:** Enter 17-digit VIN (or leave blank for now)
6. **License Plate:** ABC-1234
7. **Mileage:** 45000
8. **Color:** Silver
9. Click **Create Vehicle**

✅ **Success:** Vehicle is now linked to customer

---

## Part 4: Create an Appointment (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Navigate to Appointments

1. Click **Appointments** in sidebar
2. Click **+ New Appointment**

### Step 2: Schedule the Service

1. **Customer:** Select "John Doe"
2. **Vehicle:** Select "Toyota Camry"
3. **Service Type:** Oil Change
4. **Date:** Select tomorrow's date
5. **Time:** 10:00 AM
6. **Notes:** Regular maintenance
7. Click **Create Appointment**

✅ **Success:** Appointment created and customer receives confirmation email

---

## Part 5: Create a Job Card (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Convert Appointment to Job

1. Go to **Appointments**
2. Find the appointment you just created
3. Click **Start Job** button

OR

### Step 1b: Create Job Directly

1. Click **Job Cards** in sidebar
2. Click **+ New Job Card**

### Step 2: Fill Job Details

1. **Customer:** John Doe
2. **Vehicle:** Toyota Camry
3. **Service Type:** Oil Change
4. **Description:** Regular oil change service
5. **Assigned To:** Select a technician
6. **Estimated Time:** 1 hour
7. Click **Create Job**

✅ **Success:** Job card is created with status "Pending"

---

## Part 6: Complete a Service (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Start the Job

1. Go to **Job Cards**
2. Find your job
3. Click **Start Job**
4. Status changes to "In Progress"

### Step 2: Add Parts

1. In the job card, click **Add Parts**
2. Search for "Oil Filter"
3. Select item, enter quantity: 1
4. Click **Add**
5. Repeat for "Engine Oil" - quantity: 4 quarts

### Step 3: Add Labor

1. Click **Add Labor**
2. Enter hours: 1.0
3. Rate: $50/hour (or your rate)
4. Click **Add**

### Step 4: Complete the Job

1. Click **Complete Job**
2. Review total cost
3. Click **Confirm**

✅ **Success:** Job is completed, invoice is auto-generated

---

## Part 7: Create an Invoice (Optional - Auto-generated)

If the invoice wasn't auto-generated:

1. Go to **Invoices**
2. Click **+ New Invoice**
3. **Customer:** John Doe
4. **Job Card:** Select the completed job
5. System fills in items and labor automatically
6. **Due Date:** 30 days from today
7. Click **Generate Invoice**

✅ **Success:** Invoice is created and can be sent to customer

## Common Workflows

### Quick Workflow: Walk-in Customer

```

```

## Tips & Tricks

### ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Ctrl + K` - Quick search (find anything fast)
- `Ctrl + Shift + N` - New customer/job (context-aware)
- `Ctrl + /` - Help menu
- `Esc` - Close any modal

### 🔍 Quick Search

1. Press `Ctrl + K` anywhere

## Part 8: Accounting Basics (10 minutes)

### Step 1: View General Ledger

1. Click **General Ledger** in the Billing & Payments section
2. Browse the chart of accounts
3. Click any account to see transaction history

### Step 2: Create a Journal Entry

1. Go to **Journal Entries**
2. Click **+ New Entry**
3. Fill in:
   - **Date:** Today's date
   - **Description:** Sample transaction
   - **Debit Account:** Cash
   - **Credit Account:** Revenue
   - **Amount:** 1000
4. Click **Post Entry**

✅ **Success:** Entry posted to General Ledger

### Step 3: View Financial Reports

1. **Trial Balance:** Check that debits = credits
2. **Income Statement:** View revenue and expenses
3. **Balance Sheet:** See assets, liabilities, equity

### Step 4: Track Receivables

1. Go to **Accounts Receivable**
2. View customer aging (who owes money)
3. Click a customer to see outstanding invoices

### Step 5: Manage Budgets

1. Go to **Budget Management**
2. View existing budgets
3. Compare actual vs budgeted amounts

## Accounting Quick Reference

| Module | Purpose | Key Actions |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| General Ledger | Chart of accounts | View balances, transaction history |
| Journal Entries | Record transactions | Create, approve, reverse entries |
| Trial Balance | Verify accuracy | Check debits = credits |
| Income Statement | Profit/Loss | View revenue, expenses, net income |
| Balance Sheet | Financial position | Assets, liabilities, equity |
| Cash Flow | Cash movements | Operating, investing, financing |
| Accounts Receivable | Money owed to you | Customer aging, collections |
| Accounts Payable | Money you owe | Vendor invoices, payments |
| Cost Centers | Track costs | Department/project costs |
| Budget Management | Plan finances | Budget vs actual, forecasts |

## Troubleshooting

**Can't find a customer?**
- Use search box at top of customer list
- Try searching by phone or email

**Job not saving?**
- Check all required fields are filled
- Ensure vehicle is linked to customer
- Try refreshing the page

**Invoice not generating?**
- Ensure job is marked "Completed"
- Check that parts/labor are added
- Verify customer has email address
