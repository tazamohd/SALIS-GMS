# Cookie Policy — SALIS AUTO

**Last updated:** June 2026

SALIS AUTO uses a minimal set of cookies. We do **not** use third-party
advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

## Cookies we set

| Cookie | Type | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `connect.sid` | Strictly necessary | Maintains your authenticated session after login | Session / up to 7 days |
| CSRF token (in session) | Strictly necessary | Protects form submissions against cross-site request forgery | Session |
| Theme / language preference | Functional | Remembers your dark-mode and Arabic/English choice | Local storage |

The session cookie is `httpOnly` (not readable by JavaScript), `secure` in
production (sent only over HTTPS), and `sameSite=lax` to limit cross-site
exposure.

## What we do not use

- No advertising cookies.
- No cross-site behavioural tracking.
- No third-party analytics by default. If a garage enables an optional
  analytics integration in the future, this policy will be updated and consent
  obtained.

## Managing cookies

The strictly-necessary cookies are required for the application to function
(you cannot log in without the session cookie). You can clear cookies through
your browser settings, but doing so will sign you out.

Because we use only strictly-necessary and functional cookies, no consent
banner is legally required for tracking — however, we display a brief notice on
first visit for transparency.
