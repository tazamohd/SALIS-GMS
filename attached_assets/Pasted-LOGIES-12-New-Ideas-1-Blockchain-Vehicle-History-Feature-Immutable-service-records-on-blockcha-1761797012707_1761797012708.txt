LOGIES (12 New Ideas)
1. Blockchain Vehicle History
Feature: Immutable service records on blockchain
Benefits: Tamper-proof history, increased vehicle resale value, customer trust
Use Case: Certified pre-owned vehicles, fraud prevention
2. Augmented Reality (AR) Repair Guide
Feature: AR overlays showing repair procedures on real vehicles
Benefits: Train technicians faster, reduce errors, visual step-by-step guidance
Use Case: Complex repairs, new technician training
3. IoT Sensor Integration
Feature: Connect vehicle IoT sensors for real-time diagnostics
Benefits: Predictive alerts before breakdowns, remote monitoring
Use Case: Fleet vehicles, connected cars
4. 3D Parts Visualization
Feature: 3D models of parts and assemblies
Benefits: Better customer understanding, accurate part identification
Use Case: Complex repairs, customer approval
5. Drone Inspection Services
Feature: Drone-based vehicle exterior inspection
Benefits: Faster inspections, detailed roof/undercarriage photos
Use Case: Large vehicles, accident documentation
6. AI Video Analysis
Feature: AI analyzes uploaded videos of vehicle issues
Benefits: Remote pre-diagnosis, triage appointments
Use Case: Customer uploads video, AI suggests possible issues
7. Digital Twin Technology
Feature: Create digital replicas of customer vehicles
Benefits: Test repairs virtually, predict wear patterns
Use Case: Fleet management, preventive maintenance
8. Machine Learning Fraud Detection
Feature: AI detects fraudulent warranty claims or theft
Benefits: Reduce losses, protect business
Use Case: Parts theft, false claims
9. Biometric Authentication
Feature: Fingerprint/face recognition for technicians
Benefits: Enhanced security, accurate time tracking
Use Case: Tool cage access, job assignment
10. 5G Real-Time Collaboration
Feature: Live video consultation with remote experts
Benefits: Solve complex issues faster, knowledge sharing
Use Case: Difficult diagnostics, specialist consultation
11. Edge Computing Diagnostics
Feature: Process diagnostic data at the edge for instant results
Benefits: Faster diagnostics, work offline
Use Case: Remote locations, unreliable internet
12. Quantum Computing Price Optimization
Feature: Quantum algorithms for dynamic pricing
Benefits: Maximize revenue, competitive pricing
Use Case: Real-time market-based pricing
