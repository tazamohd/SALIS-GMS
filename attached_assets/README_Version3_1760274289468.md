# 🚗 Garage Analysis – Complete Documentation & Architecture

## Overview

**Garage Analysis** is a modular SaaS platform designed for automotive garages in the Middle East. It enables end-to-end digital management of spare parts, services, appointments, technician workflows, inventory, job tracking, billing, and customer engagement—supporting both small workshops and large multi-branch chains.

## Business Context & Vision

- **Goal:** Digitize and streamline the entire lifecycle of automotive garage operations, with a focus on localization (Arabic/English, regional currencies), multi-branch scalability, and role-based access.
- **Target Users:** Garage owners, staff (receptionists, tech leaders, purchasers, invoice managers, technicians), customers, insurance partners, SaaS admins.
- **Channels:** Web dashboards, technician apps, customer apps.
- **Business Model:** Subscription-based, with support for add-on modules and usage-based billing.

---

## Core Architecture & Entity Overview

- **Multi-Tenant:** Each garage operates as a tenant, with one or more branches.
- **Branch Separation:** Inventory, staff, appointments, and settings are branch-specific; data can be shared as needed.
- **Role-Based Access:** Fixed roles (owner, admin, manager, technician, receptionist, etc.); users can belong to multiple branches.
- **Audit & Compliance:** All critical actions are audit-logged; support for regulatory and business reporting.

## Functional Modules

### Garage & Branch Management

- **Creation:** Garage and branch creation with required (and optional) fields; status lifecycle (`Pending`, `Active`, `Suspended`, `Archived`).
- **Approval:** SaaS admin must approve new garages; document upload and verification.
- **Branch Inheritance:** Working hours, contact info, branding, service types inherited from garage; per-branch overrides.
- **Custom Fields:** Admin-defined fields for garage, branch, and staff metadata.

### User & Role Management

- **Roles:** Predefined roles (see [User-Role-Management.md](docs/Identify-functional-modules/User-Role-Management.md)); one role per branch, multi-branch support.
- **Onboarding:** Admin invite, self-registration with admin approval.
- **Lifecycle:** Deactivation on last branch removal; impersonation, account expiry, MFA support.
- **Permissions:** Hardcoded per role, with a permission matrix.
- **Session/Activity Logs:** Full audit of logins, impersonations, role changes.

### Service Templates & Step Workflow

- **ServiceTemplate:** Defines reusable service processes; supports skill-based step assignment, vehicle/market targeting, pricing modes, garage overrides.
- **ServiceStepTemplate:** Each step has skills, tools, parts, validations (photos, checklist, approval), and audit logging.
- **Step Execution:** Status transitions, validation, and time tracking per technician/assistant.

### Spare Parts & Inventory

- **SparePart:** Metadata, compatibility, tool/service linkages, tags, global/shared/local visibility.
- **SparePartInventory:** Per-garage/branch stock, pricing, min thresholds, taxes, override logic.
- **Bulk Import/Export:** Excel-based workflows; simulated TecDoc API enrichment.
- **Filters & Badges:** Rich filtering, UI tags for source/import status, soft delete.

### Tool Management

- **Tool:** Metadata, compatibility, service/part linkages, media, documentation, global/local scope.
- **ToolAvailability:** Per-garage/branch quantity and status tracking.
- **Linking:** Tools linked to services and parts; assignment managed via API and UI.

### Job Cards & Progress Tracking

- **JobCard:** Auto-created at check-in; step-by-step assignment, status, checklist, exceptions.
- **Assignment:** Skill, workload, and availability-driven; supports parallel/sequential flows.
- **Exception Handling:** Approval and rework logic; full audit of step completion and exceptions.

### Appointments & Check-in

- **Booking:** Customer-facing UI, real-time slot and technician availability.
- **Check-in:** Photo capture, optional vehicle condition logging, add-on suggestions, intake printout, notification flow.
- **Rejection Approval:** Late/incorrect/incompatible arrivals require admin approval.

### Customer Management

- **Profiles:** Name, contact, vehicles, preferences, service/invoice history.
- **Feedback:** Survey, complaint, and loyalty logic.
- **Communication:** Notifications and reminders.

### Purchase Orders & Supplier Integration

- **PO Creation:** Manual/auto part and tool restocking, multi-vendor support, approval flow.
- **Supplier Directory:** Vendor management, price tracking, and PO lifecycle.

### Invoice & Billing

- **Invoice Generation:** From jobs, with parts, labor, tax, and discount logic.
- **Payment Tracking:** Status, receipts, and integration with accounting.

### Reports & Dashboards

- **Analytics:** Revenue, technician utilization, inventory turnover, customer satisfaction.
- **Exports:** CSV/PDF, configurable by role and module.

### Mobile Apps Integration

- **Technician App:** Job flows, timer, checklist, media upload, notifications, offline support.
- **Customer App:** Booking, approvals, status tracking, payment, push notifications.
