Platform Features

Real-Time Service Bay Dashboard

Live bay status monitoring with WebSocket updates, technician assignments, and job progress tracking for optimal workshop efficiency.

AI-Powered Inventory Management

Automated reordering with predictive demand forecasting, supplier integration, and smart stock level optimization.

Customer Loyalty Program

Points accumulation, tier progression (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum), rewards redemption, and referral bonuses.

Interactive Workshop Calendar

Visual calendar with drag-and-drop job scheduling, technician availability tracking, and resource allocation.

Augmented Reality for Mechanics

AR-assisted repair guides, parts identification, and step-by-step visual instructions for complex repairs.

OBD Diagnostics Integration

Real-time vehicle diagnostics, fault code reading, live sensor data, and automated diagnostic reports.

Multi-Franchise Management

Centralized command center for franchise networks with KPI tracking, revenue sharing, and performance analytics.

Saudi Arabia Compliance

Full VAT, ZATCA E-Invoicing, Zakat integration, Hijri calendar, Arabic language, and TRN validation support.
