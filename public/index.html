Platform

Every part of the garage, finally talking to itself.

Eight pillars covering everything from the moment a customer books to the moment the invoice clears.

Real-Time Service Bay Dashboard

Live bay status with WebSocket updates, technician assignments, and job progress — see workshop load at a glance.

AI-Powered Inventory Management

Automated reordering with predictive demand forecasting, supplier integration, and smart stock-level optimization.

Customer Loyalty Programs

Tiered points (Bronze → Platinum), referral bonuses, redemption flows — built to keep customers coming back.

Interactive Workshop Calendar

Visual drag-and-drop scheduling, technician availability tracking, and resource allocation across bays.

OBD Diagnostics Integration

Real-time vehicle telemetry, fault-code reading, live sensor data, and automated diagnostic reports.

Augmented Reality for Mechanics

AR-assisted repair guides, parts identification, and step-by-step visual instructions — even for first-day technicians.

Multi-Franchise Management

Centralized command center for franchise networks with KPI tracking, revenue sharing, and performance analytics.

Saudi Arabia Compliance

Full VAT, ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing, Zakat integration, Hijri calendar, Arabic UI, and TRN validation.
